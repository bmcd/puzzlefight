Welcome to Puzzle Fight

Puzzle Fight is a competitive Tetris-like puzzle game for two players. It was built using Javascript and the HTML5 Canvas, with a Node.js back-end for online play.

The goal is to fill up your opponent's screen by eliminating blocks on your side. Land a circle "breaker" next to a group of blocks of the same color to destroy them all and send them over to your opponent's board. Don't let your screen fill up!
